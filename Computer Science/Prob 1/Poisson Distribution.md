## 4. Poisson Distribution
> F(x) = e^-λ . λ^x/x! 
>  X ~ poisson(λ)
> Proof is literally Mclaurin of e^x
> E(X) = λ
> V(X) = λ
> λ -> average success rate
> n is not known here -> tends to infinity
> Can add lambdas in different intervals
Ex: λ = 1 -> [8,9] and λ = 3 -> [9,10]
λ [8,10] -> 1 + 3
