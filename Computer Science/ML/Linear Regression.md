### AME
1/m times Summtion of Abs(y-Y)

y: Actual
Y: Prediction


### MSE
1/m times Summtion of (Y-y)^2

y: Actual
Y: Prediction

### Get linear line
y = w1X+w2

y= (w1+px)x + (w2+px)
w1: Slope
w2: Interception
